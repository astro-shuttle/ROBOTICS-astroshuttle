steerL [ANGLE] – servo turn left to angle
steerR [ANGLE] – servo turn right to angle
steerL - servo turn to default angle
steer - servo turn to default angle
steerZ - zero the servos

driveF – drive forward
driveB – drive backward
DdriveL –  servo-based turn left (with differential drive)
DdriveR – servo-based turn right (with differential drive)

skidL – skid steer left (in-place turn) [DC only]
skidR – skid steer right (in-place turn) [DC only]

S - stop motors 
C - show commands
