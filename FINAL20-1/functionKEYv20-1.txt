steerL [ANGLE] – servo turn left to a specified sangle
steerR [ANGLE] – servo turn right to a specified angle
steerZ - zero the servos

driveF – drive forward
driveB – drive backward

DdriveL – differential drive left
DdriveR – differential drive right

skidL – skid steer left (in-place turn) [DC only]
skidR – skid steer right (in-place turn) [DC only]

S - stop motors 
C - show commands
